Our game features custom assets build in blender. We have tried to stay very low-poly in the desing, as a design choise, but also due to the time limit. These are coloursed in unity with non-texture materials.

This is where you start - right outside your tent. This tent has been with you for ages and despite its small appearance, can fit a cube of two as It is bigger on the inside.

The tree was one of our first assets. This serves you as your first trail and introduction to the crafting. For you to proceed you have to gain a wooden plank from the tree.

This tree can only be chopped with a tool made for wood. This axe can be found lying around your home base.

By combining your newly chopped wood with some rope you craft a bridge - finaly you can reach out to new biomes. This rope was built by small circles twirled together to a rope in blender.

This is your creation. Be proud.

Rocks are essential to all crafting games. Smash it for some ore.

This simple pickaxe can smash rock.

The ore can then be melted by the fire. This fire is animated and sparks off fire particles everywhere!

This flower starts of as a small little bud. With you aid and some water it will grow and bridge between two biomes - creating a natural 100% vegan bridge.
